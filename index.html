Do I need to know how to code?

No. You drive it through your AI agent in plain language. Basic familiarity with Roblox Studio helps you check the results.

How does the free trial work?

Install and use everything free for 14 days, no credit card. After that, subscribe and paste your license key to keep going.

Which AI assistants are supported?

Any MCP-compatible client: Claude Code, Claude Desktop, Codex, Cursor, Windsurf, Antigravity / Gemini, and Cline.

Can I use one license on several computers?

A license activates on one device at a time. Moving to a new machine is supported; reach out if you need to free up an activation.

What happens if I cancel?

You keep access until the end of your billing period. After that the license stops validating and the tools lock.
